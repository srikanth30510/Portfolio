Online Job Consulting System

  • by:Srikanth Godavari
  • team member:Shaik Muzamil Rehaman
  • time taken:3 months

Embarking on the digital landscape, I spearheaded the development of SS Consultancy, an innovative Online Job Consulting System. Leveraging the power of the Django framework, this project revolutionizes the traditional job consulting process, bringing efficiency and accessibility to both job seekers and employers.

Key Features:

User-Friendly Interface: SS Consultancy boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, ensuring a seamless experience for both job seekers and employers.

Dynamic Job Listings: Through the dynamic job listing feature, job seekers can explore a myriad of opportunities, while employers can effortlessly post and manage job openings.

Resume Upload and Management: The platform facilitates job seekers in uploading and managing their resumes, streamlining the application process.

Advanced Search and Filtering: With advanced search and filtering options, users can narrow down job listings based on their preferences, saving time and enhancing the job search experience.

Secure User Authentication: Prioritizing user security, SS Consultancy integrates robust authentication mechanisms to safeguard sensitive information.

Tech Stack:

Framework: Django

Database: PostgreSQL

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Authentication: Django Authentication System
